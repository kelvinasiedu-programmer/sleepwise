About SleepWise

Last reviewed: June 2026

SleepWise is a free educational tool that checks common sleep supplements against the medications and supplements you already take, plus a few health flags, and surfaces interaction concerns and evidence before you buy anything.

Why it exists

Most "best sleep supplement" content ignores the question people actually worry about: can I safely take this with what I already take? SleepWise is built around that question. It is a safety layer between supplement curiosity and a purchase, and it is designed to help you prepare better questions for a pharmacist or clinician.

What it does

  • Maps your medications to drug classes and checks them against a curated interaction table.
  • Flags additive sedation when several sedating supplements would be combined.
  • Shows evidence and source links for each result, drawn from public medical databases.

What it does not do

  • It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
  • It does not replace your doctor or pharmacist.
  • It does not cover every medication, supplement, or interaction.

Who built it

SleepWise was built by Kelvin Asiedu as an independent educational project. It has not yet been reviewed by a licensed clinician or pharmacist; see Methodology for how recommendations are generated and where the limits are.
