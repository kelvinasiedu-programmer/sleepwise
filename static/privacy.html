Privacy

Last reviewed: June 2026

SleepWise is built for data minimization. The short version: no account, no stored medication lists, no advertising trackers.

What we do not do

  • We do not require an account.
  • We do not save your medication list, supplement list, or health flags.
  • We do not sell or share your entries.
  • We do not run advertising pixels or third-party trackers on pages where you enter health information.

What actually happens to your entries

When you submit the form, your entries are sent to the server only to generate the result on that page, and are not written to a database. The server keeps standard operational logs (timestamp, page, response status, response time) that do not include the medications or supplements you typed. Results may be held briefly in server memory to speed up identical repeat checks; that cache is never written to disk and clears when the service restarts.

Affiliate links

"Find this supplement" links currently point to general supplement searches and are marked as sponsored/no-follow. They are not yet monetized affiliate links. If that changes, it will be disclosed clearly here and on the result, and product suggestions will never be ranked by payout.

Optional integrations

If error monitoring is enabled by the operator, technical error reports may be sent to a third-party service. These reports are intended to capture software errors, not your health entries.
